# The Dollhouse

## Maxwell

  - Human, male.
  - An eccentric artificer and researcher that loves his dolls as his
    daughters.
  - As the sole son of one of the thirteen noblemen governors, he
    unwillingly inherited the duty of securing and maintaining the royal
    mausoleum.
